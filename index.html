Tip Tracker

Keep tabs on just how much you're spending on your nights out.

View on the App Store

What does it do?

Tip Tracker provides you with a simple way of tracking your spending on nights out, as we all know that feeling when you’re waiting on your next pay check to cover living costs after spending a little too much in a month.

Tip Tracker offers the following capabilities:

  • Calculate the bill total, including any tip, using the tip slider
  • Keep track of your spending on dining out
  • Keep a record of all the different places you've ever been too!

Keeping track of your dining expenditure will allow you to ensure you’re not overindulging, and see at a glance exactly how much you’ve spent lately. And remember all the great (and not so great) places you've ever been too, and exactly where they were!

Enjoy the experience of dining out, knowing you’ll still have enough money to spend on the necessities at the end of the month.

Please note, none of this is financial advice. We simply provide a tool to put you in control.

How do I use it?

Step 1 - Fill in your bill details while your location is automatically retrieved

Step 2 - Drag the slider to adjust the tip amount and calculate the grand total

Step 3 - Hit 'Keep Receipt' to save it to your wallet

Step 4 - See all your saved bills and exactly how much you've spent on dining out

Step 5 - Tap on a receipt to see more details

Step 6 - Swipe to delete any old receipts

Step 7 - Hit the X to close your wallet

Step 8 - To re-open your wallet at any point hit the folder icon in the upper left corner
